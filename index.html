> Compatibility

Exactlink comes with a DB25 connector for a RS232 interface. Tested on Fanuc controlled machines, and follows the RS232 standard. The desktop application is available for Windows and Linux systems, designed with both tablets and desktops in mind.

How to use.

The Exactlink device is connected to the machine and to a tablet or PC with a USB cable. When connected you can simply transfer any file from the application.
